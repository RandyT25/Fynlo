Your data lives in a cloud database (Supabase / PostgreSQL) so it's accessible from any device. Accounts are secured with email/password login. You can request full deletion at any time by contacting support.

Completely free. No subscription, no trial period, no credit card required. Create an account and start tracking immediately — all features included.

Install Guide

Add to your home screen

No app store needed. Install Fynlo in seconds — it feels exactly like a native app.

iPhone / iPad

Safari required

  1. 1

    Open in Safari

    Visit fynlo-kappa.vercel.app in Safari (not Chrome or other browsers)

  2. 2

    Tap the Share button

    The square with an arrow icon at the bottom of Safari

  3. 3

    Tap "Add to Home Screen"

    Scroll down in the share sheet and tap this option

  4. 4

    Tap "Add"

    Fynlo appears on your home screen and opens fullscreen — no browser bar

Android

Chrome recommended

  1. 1

    Open in Chrome

    Visit fynlo-kappa.vercel.app in Chrome

  2. 2

    Tap the menu (⋮)

    Three-dot menu in the top right corner of Chrome

  3. 3

    Tap "Add to Home screen"

    Or you may see a banner at the bottom automatically offering to install

  4. 4

    Tap "Install"

    Fynlo installs like a native app with its own icon and fullscreen experience
