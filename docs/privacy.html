Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

Fynlo collects only the information you voluntarily provide when creating an account and using the app:

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Financial data you enter manually (transactions, accounts, budgets, goals)
  • App usage data for improving the product (anonymized)

We do not connect to your bank accounts, read SMS, or access any financial institution on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to provide the Fynlo service to you. We do not sell, share, or trade your personal information with any third party. Your financial data is yours alone.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security enabled. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We follow industry-standard security practices.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

5. Cookies

Fynlo uses session cookies strictly for authentication purposes. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

6. Children's Privacy

Fynlo is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify users of significant changes via the app or email.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at support@fynlo.app
